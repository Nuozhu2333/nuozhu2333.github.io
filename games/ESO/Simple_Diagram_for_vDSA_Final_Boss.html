Simple Diagram for vDSA Final Boss

Dragonstar Arena is not hard at all. However, it is really L-O-N-G. So usually, a team composed of one tank and three damage dealers is more efficent and time-saving in farming. Here is a simple diagram to explain how to defeat the final boss quickly and painlessly.

Requirement: The total dps is recommended to exceed 100k. One of the DDs can taunt the boss. Both tank and one of the DDs can give Barrier.

Area #1: The DDs should stay beside the gate before the boss arrives

Reviving Barrier is better than Replenishing Barrier in this situation, since it's a healing over time.
