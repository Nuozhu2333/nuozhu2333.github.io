Simple Diagram for vDSA Final Boss

Dragonstar Arena is not difficult, but it is very long. Therefore, a team composed of one tank and three damage dealers (DDs) is usually more efficient and time-saving for farming. Here is a simple diagram to explain how to defeat the final boss quickly and easily.

Requirements: The total DPS should exceed 100k. One of the DDs can taunt the boss. Both the tank and one of the DDs should be able to provide a Barrier.

Area #1: The DDs should stay beside the gate before the boss arrives.

Area #2: The tank should stay here with the boss until the first Flame Atronach appears.

Area #3: The tank should stay here with the adds and prevent them from attacking the DDs.


At the beginning, the DDs should stay in Area #1, and the tank should go to Area #2 and wait for the boss. When the boss appears, the tank taunts him and stays in Area #2 until the first Flame Atronach emerges. Afterward, the tank moves to Area #1, and one of the DDs will taunt the boss.

When the boss reaches 75% health, he will summon adds. The tank needs to taunt them and bring them to Area #3.

When the boss reaches 50% health, he will pull all players toward him and then release a full-screen AoE attack. The tank needs to return to Area #1 before this happens, and the DD should provide a Barrier. After that, the tank should return to Area #3.

When the boss reaches 25% health, the same thing will happen again, but this time the tank should provide the Barrier. Afterward, the tank returns to Area #3 again, and the DDs finish off the boss. Not hard, right? But there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. The tank needs to stay in Area #2, not Area #3, before the first Flame Atronach appears to avoid the fire AoE.

2. The tank does not need to provide any buffs—just focus on surviving and avoid dying.

3. Reviving Barrier is better than Replenishing Barrier in this situation, as it is a HoT.

4. When there is a healer in the group, they will be responsible for taunting the boss and providing the Barrier. Heal-over-time abilities are essential!
